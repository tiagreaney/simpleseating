SimpleSeating

An App for Scheduling Hot Desks

For Companies

You can sign up your company for FREE. You can share UPDATES with your employees. You can ADD and DELETE SEATS according to a SEATING PLAN of your office that you can upload. You can view the BOOKINGS made by your employees.

Sign up »

For Employees

You can register as an employee for FREE. You can view your company's UPDATES and SEATING PLAN. You can BOOK a seat and view upcoming, past and today's BOOKINGS.

Register »

Features

Filter by department Co-ordinate with your team

You can filter seat bookings by department. Employees can view seats and the time/date people in certain departments have booked. With this feature, employees can coordinate with their team members and collaborate together at the office.

Update your NewsfeedIt'll keep your employees up to date.

A company can post an update to be displayed on their employees newsfeed. Important messages can instantaneously be displayed to employees.

Email confirmation You won't forget

An email is sent to the employee when they reserve or cancel a seat booking. Should the company delete a seat or booking the employee will be notified via email.
